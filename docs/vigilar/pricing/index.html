Vigilar — Pricing

Core security is always free. AI is the upsell. No ads, no tracking, no data collection — on any tier.

🔒 Our Promise
Every tier — including Free — has zero ads, zero tracking, and zero data collection. Your security is not for sale.

Free

Everything you need to monitor your security posture, at no cost.

  • Security Dashboard — Gatekeeper, SIP, FileVault, Firewall, XProtect status
  • Breach Monitor — 5 checks per day (HIBP API, k-anonymity)
  • Threat Detection — Suspicious processes, launch agents, open ports, app audit
  • Security Score — Weighted assessment with actionable recommendations

Price: Free, forever. No account required.

Pro — €9.99/month or €89.99/year

Everything in Free, plus AI-powered security guidance and enhanced protection.

  • AI Companion Chat — Unlimited conversations with the on-device 12B model
  • Priority Breach Alerts — Real-time push notifications when your data appears in a breach
  • Identity Theft Protection — Offline heuristic analysis of exposure vectors
  • ARCZT Vault Link — Deep-link bridge to ARCZT Personal Vault for breach-to-safe matching

Price: €9.99/month or €89.99/year (save 25% with annual). License is per-device.

Family — €19.99/month or €179.99/year

Protect the whole household. Everything in Pro, multiplied across devices.

  • 5 Device Licenses — Cover up to 5 devices (macOS and Android)
  • Shared Breach Monitoring — Monitor multiple email addresses across family members
  • Family Security Dashboard — Unified view of all family devices and breach status

Price: €19.99/month or €179.99/year (save 25% with annual). Covers 5 devices.

Lifetime — €199.99 (One-Time Purchase)

Pay once, use forever. No subscription, no recurring payments.

  • Everything in Pro
  • Single device license — one-time activation, no expiration
  • No subscription required

Price: €199.99 one-time payment. Single device only.

💡 Licensing Notes
  • Licenses are per-device. Each device needs its own license.
  • The Family plan covers 5 devices under one subscription.
  • Core security features are always free — dashboard, breach monitor, threat detection, and security score. AI chat is the upsell.
  • No ads, no tracking, no data collection on any tier.
