How they connect

Two standalone apps, one deep-link bridge. No shared data, no shared backend.

🔥

Vigilar detects a breach

Breach monitor checks your email against known breach databases. When credentials are exposed, Vigilar sends breach metadata to ARCZT.

🔗

Deep-link bridge

Only breach metadata flows between apps — domain, breach name, date, severity. No credentials, no passwords, no entry names. Ever.

🔑

ARCZT links to your safe

ARCZT matches the breached domain against entries in your safe. Shows you which entries are linked to that breach — all locally, all private.
