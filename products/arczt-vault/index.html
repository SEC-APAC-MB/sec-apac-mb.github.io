ARCZT Personal Vault

Zero-knowledge, quantum-resistant credential management. Your safe never leaves your device.

What is ARCZT?

ARCZT Personal Vault is a standalone credential manager. It stores your passwords, API keys, and sensitive entries in an encrypted safe that lives only on your device. No cloud sync, no server, no account required.

🔐 Zero-knowledge
Your safe is encrypted with keys derived from your master password. ARCZT cannot decrypt your data — we don't have your key and we don't want it.

Key Features

  • Encrypted Safe — AES-256-GCM encryption with Argon2id key derivation
  • Password Generator — Cryptographically secure, configurable strength and character sets
  • MFA & SSO — Multi-factor authentication for vault access, SSO integration
  • Credential Sharing — Share entries with end-to-end encryption
  • Audit Log — Tracks entry creation, access, and changes
  • Breach-Aware — Links safe entries to known breaches via Vigilar integration
  • Browser Extension — Autofill from your vault without exposing the safe

Vigilar Integration

ARCZT and Vigilar are separate apps connected by a deep-link bridge. When Vigilar detects a breach, it sends only breach metadata (domain, breach name, date, severity) to ARCZT — never any credentials. ARCZT then shows you which entries in your safe are linked to that breach.

No credentials flow between the two apps. The connection is metadata-only.

Security Model

  • Encryption: AES-256-GCM (authenticated encryption)
  • Key derivation: Argon2id (memory-hard, side-channel resistant)
  • Quantum resistance: Symmetric AES-256 is quantum-resistant (Grover's algorithm reduces to 128-bit security)
  • Master password: Never stored, never transmitted. Used only to derive the encryption key.
  • Auto-lock: Configurable timeout, biometric re-unlock (Face ID, fingerprint)
  • Zero network: No telemetry, no sync, no cloud. The safe is local only.

Technical Specs

  • Backend: Python (FastAPI), PostgreSQL, Redis
  • Frontend: Next.js + TypeScript
  • Mobile: Android (Kotlin), iOS (Swift)
  • Deployment: Docker Compose (self-hosted) or standalone desktop app
  • Database: arczt_vault — encrypted at rest
