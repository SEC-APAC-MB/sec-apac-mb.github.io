About SEC-APAC-MB

We build on-device cybersecurity products. No cloud, no tracking, no compromise.

Who we are

SEC-APAC-MB is a cybersecurity company building on-device security products. We believe security tools should work without sending your data to the cloud — because the safest data is data that never left your device.

Our products — Vigilar and ARCZT Personal Vault — are standalone apps that run entirely locally. No cloud servers, no data collection, no behavioral tracking. Just security that works on your device, for your data.

Our principles

On-device first

Everything runs locally. Your data never touches a server you don't control.

Zero-knowledge by default

We can't see your data. We don't want to. Encryption is the floor, not the ceiling.

No tracking, no ads

We sell software, not your attention. No analytics, no profiling, no brokering.

Open source where it matters

Our products are built on Apache 2.0 licensed components. Source code is available on GitHub.

Products, not lock-in

You buy our apps. You own them. No cloud dependency, no forced subscriptions.

Security over convenience

We make security usable, but we never trade safety for a smoother onboarding.
